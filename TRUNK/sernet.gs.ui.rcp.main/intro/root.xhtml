Welcome to verinice!


This tool will assist you in implementing and maintaining an information security management system (ISMS) based on national and international standards such as ISO 27001. It will help you to carry out and document the necessary steps but it does not replace the necessary knowledge and management skills of the information security officer! verinice is an expert tool, designed to assist CISOs as well as internal and external auditors in their daily work.

If you have any problems or questions, free community support for verinice is available by email. Please do not hesitate to tell us about your opinion at verinice@sernet.com. Help is also available in our FAQ at http://verinice.org.

If you need professional support, wish to customize verinice to your company's needs or if you have questions about ISMS implementation and operation you can request SerNet's professional support by visiting our website http://sernet.com. A highly skilled staff of information security practitioners and auditors is ready to assist you.
